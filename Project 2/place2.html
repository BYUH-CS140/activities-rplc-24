Mambukal Resort is a mountain resort nestled in the foothills of Mount Kanlaon. Known for its hot springs and waterfalls, it's a favorite weekend getaway for both locals and tourists seeking relaxation and adventure in nature. Situated 1,200 ft. above sea level and next to Mount Kanlaon, Mambukal enjoys cool temperatures all year round with access to flora and fauna native to the Kanlaon area.
