How to Make Pork Sinigang

What You'll Need (Ingredients):

  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced into cubes
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 medium taro roots (gabi), peeled and halved
  • 1 packet of Sinigang mix
  • 8 cups of water
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

What To Do (Instructions):

  1. Boil the Pork: In a large pot, add the water, onion, and tomatoes. Bring it to a boil. Add the pork cubes and let it simmer for 45 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  2. Add the Flavor: Pour in the Sinigang mix and stir well. Add the taro roots (gabi) and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add Veggies: Add the kangkong and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the leaves are wilted.
  4. Season: Add a splash of fish sauce (patis) for extra flavor. Serve hot with a bowl of rice. Enjoy!
